Convolution:

 How to use:
This WebApp illustrate the concept of convolution. You can pick two functions in the select boxes and see interactively their convolution.
Pick the functions and move the mouse in the second line plot to slide one over the other. The product of the displaced and mirrored versiob of $f(x)$ and $g(x)$ will be shown in the third plot line and convolution will be shown in the last one.

Press OK to see the function of each element in the WebApp.
